# Excluded Documents

Documents considered for inclusion but excluded due to restricted or ambiguous distribution status. Per project rules: ambiguous licensing = exclude (conservative default).

## FM 3-05.70 - Survival (May 2002)

- **Reason:** Restricted distribution -- "Distribution authorized to U.S. Government agencies and their contractors only" + NOFORN marking
- **Verified at:** armypubs.army.mil (PUB_ID=78014, marked INACTIVE, NOFORN)
- **Verification date:** 2026-02-28
- **Notes:** FM 3-05.70 superseded FM 21-76 (1992) but carries a restricted distribution statement. Despite being widely available commercially and on third-party websites, the official Army Publishing Directorate record confirms it is NOT approved for public release. Commercial availability does not equal public release authorization.
- **Alternative included:** FM 21-76 (1992) covers same survival topics with Distribution Statement A ("Approved for public release; distribution is unlimited")

## ST 31-91B - Special Forces Medical Handbook (March 1982)

- **Reason:** Ambiguous distribution status -- no official Distribution Statement A found
- **Verified at:** No armypubs.army.mil listing found; searched Army Publishing Directorate FM, TC, and ST indexes
- **Verification date:** 2026-02-28
- **Notes:** Widely commercially republished (available on Amazon, multiple websites) but no .mil or .gov verification of public release authorization could be located. The SOF Medical Handbook (2001 USSOCOM edition) also lacks clear public release verification. Per project rules, ambiguous = excluded.
- **Partial alternative:** FM 4-25.11 (First Aid, 2002) and TC 4-02.1 (First Aid, 2016) cover basic field medicine with confirmed Distribution Statement A
